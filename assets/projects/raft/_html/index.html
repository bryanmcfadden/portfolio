H4/ASI/Non-Telco Wages
Incurred wages of employees paid on Non Telco, Non Mobility payroll companies. Wages for such employees are not paid through the Telco and as a result are not included in the cost pools represented in the Capital driven force model. In MW this includes the charges using XC B52 which are excluded in the core FDW queries.
Net Cross Charges
Essentially the net of cross charging between C&E and entities like CIM. This has to tie off with input from the involved organizations. Clearances From Others Clearances associated with wages of employees paid outside the regional VP organizations but within legal entity and force group combinations included in the CADs rates of the regional VP. This includes employees assigned to an indirect cost pool that are paid through the Telco legal entities on an RC beginning with %%KA%. Such employees have incurred dollars that are budgeted under a staff organization and have clearances distributed through the productive hours of the regional C&E organization. For example, centers employees working under Nonetta Sode on RC S0KA37L00 on JFC 325A will have incurred payroll dollars budgeted in centers and labor clearance dollars budgeted under SW C&E. In this example, even though payroll is issued against S0KA37L00, the dollars fall into the indirect B33 cost pool that is distributed at the Legal Entity (SWBT) / Force Group (E03) level… meaning the dollars get added to the B33 rate that is cleared by the productive hours of the regional C&E organization
